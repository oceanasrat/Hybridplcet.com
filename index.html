About HYBRID PLC

Based in the heart of Addis Ababa, HYBRID PLC TRANSPORT & CAR RENT SERVICES is founded with a passion for sustainable travel. We are committed to providing an exceptional rental experience that's kinder to the planet. We believe that eco-friendly choices shouldn't mean compromising on quality, comfort, or price.

Our mission is to make hybrid and electric vehicles accessible to everyone in Ethiopia, from daily commuters to adventurous travelers. With a meticulously maintained fleet and a customer-first approach, we're driving the future of car rentals.
